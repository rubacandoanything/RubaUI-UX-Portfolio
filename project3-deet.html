Sale CRM Dashboard

Project: AI Powered Sales Management Platform

The Sales CRM Dashboard is a centralized platform designed to streamline lead management, team performance tracking, and sales conversion. Leads enter the system through multiple sources and are authenticated by Telecallers. Once verified, they are guided through counseling and engagement stages, ultimately converting into customers upon purchasing a course or product. The Admin oversees every step of the pipeline, with the ability to assign teams, monitor sales progress, and evaluate departmental productivity. Integrated AI continuously analyzes employee activity and sales data, generating smart reports that offer insights and personalized improvement suggestions for each team member.

AI Powered E-Book & Job Match Product Page

Project: Website Design

Designed a job-oriented e-book platform where users can explore job listings and receive AI-driven e-book recommendations based on the job’s required skills. Each job detail page includes a comprehensive job description, a search CTA, and a suggested e-book tailored to skill gaps. Clicking on the e-book reveals preview pages, pricing, and a breakdown of how the e-book can help users land their target role. The interface combines user-centric design with clean navigation to support seamless learning and career growth.

Release Date 2024.04

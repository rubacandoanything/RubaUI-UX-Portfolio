Modular IT Solutions Dashboard

Project: Landing Page Design and Referral Driven Dashboard

The Sankhyana Services Dashboard is designed to manage and track multiple business services such as AICV Solutions, Data Analytics, and IT Solutions. Each service, from resume building with AI-driven CV solutions to web development and data analytics for various industries, is accessible through a clear and intuitive dashboard interface. The dashboard also supports a referral system, allowing businesses or individuals to refer clients to the services and track their engagement. Users can view detailed information about each service, including its features, benefits, industry relevance, testimonials, and pricing. Additionally, the data analytics and IT solutions services are customized for different industries like Retail, Telecom, and Finance, ensuring that businesses can access industry-specific solutions. The dashboard serves as a one-stop solution for businesses seeking to explore, purchase, and refer services within the Sankhyana platform.

Release Date 2024.04
